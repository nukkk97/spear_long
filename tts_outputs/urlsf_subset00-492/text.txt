Dozens of women were raped in coordinated attack in the Democratic Republic of Congo on New Year Day Doctors Without Borders said Thursday The humanitarian agency said women were raped in Fizi South Kivu in the eastern part of the war torn country Women had been restrained with ropes or beaten unconscious with the butt of gun before being attacked some in front of their children said Annemarie Loof an official with the agency commonly known by its French name Medecins Sans Frontieres Up to four armed men were involved at time and homes and shops were looted she said in statement Rape is frequent weapon of war in Congo the United Nations says It has named the Democratic Republic of Congo the rape capital of the world with women raped in eastern Congo in The attacks occurred in parts of the country where armed rebel groups moved into areas considered to be pro government but lacking in army or police protection according to the United Nations Many rape survivors took to the streets in October to speak out against sexual violence My heart is in pain Why are you raping me sang the rape victims many of whom left hospital beds to join the march
